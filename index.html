Welcome to Steam Indie Fest


Your ultimate destination for discovering amazing indie games on Steam!


Steam Indie Fest brings the excitement of our popular online event, Steam Next Fest, into the real world
with a three-day, in-person celebration of upcoming indie games on Steam!


Explore the show floor and be among the first to play the latest indie titles at hands-on demo booths.


Immerse yourself in a packed schedule of live entertainment, including developer interviews,
panel discussions, roundtables, and even live concertsâ€”all happening on our main stage throughout the event.


Join us for an unforgettable weekend of games, community, and creativity at Steam Indie Fest!
