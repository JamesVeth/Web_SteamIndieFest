Welcome to Steam Indie Fest


Your ultimate destination for discovering amazing indie games on Steam!


Steam Indie Fest takes the idea of our popular online event, Steam Next Fest, and turns it into a 3 day in-person video game festival, which celebrates Indie games coming soon to Steam!


Over the course of 3 days you'll get the chance to play the latest titles at the booths on the show floor


There will also be a live stage show, which will run throughout the entirety of the show and feature shows, interview panels, round tables and even concerts!
